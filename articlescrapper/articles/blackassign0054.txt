<h1 class="entry-title">Embedding care robots into society and practice: Socio-technical considerations</h1> <div class="td-post-content tagdiv-type">
        
<p>Care Robots, as the name suggests, are robots that are used for hospitality purposes like fetching water, cracking jokes and keeping a patient in good harmony, etc.</p>



<p>The senior care industry has been at the forefront for quite a period. The reason being, Nuclear families becoming very busy in their schedule that one day when the parents of this nuclear family are<a href="https://www.empoweringparents.com/article/out-of-control-child-stopping-the-family-anxiety-cycle/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> old, children are nowhere to take care of. Instead</a>, nursing homes have become a trend.  Parents with mental illness are being put in rehab.</p>



<p>As observed for a few decades <a href="https://insights.blackcoffer.com/can-robots-tackle-late-life-loneliness/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">robots are taking care of many activities</a> and they are even dominating a few fields like the automobile industry. So, Machines can indeed help humankind in achieving remarkable success in many fields.</p>



<p>Do senior citizens <a href="https://insights.blackcoffer.com/the-workflow-of-a-machine-learning-artificial-intelligence-project/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">love machines</a> ?, You should look at them when they watch TV when they love to play video games on a smartphone. So, If we give them a human-like structure with the ability of a smartphone to make these people happy then it would be quite a monstrous feat.</p>
