<h1 class="entry-title">How we forecast future technologies?</h1> <div class="td-post-content tagdiv-type">
        
<p>TECHNOLOGY is playing a dominant role in human life. And as in our daily lifestyle technology is used each and every place where human beings are present. so How we forecast future technologies? </p>



<p>So, the fact is that technology is not a single immutable piece of hardware orbit chemistry. It is simply<a href="https://sites.kowsarpub.com/jcp/articles/63292.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> knowledge of physical relationships</a> and it systematically applied to the useful arts. This knowledge can vary continuously over time. It can range basic phenomenon can be applied to an end product, device, or production machine <a href="https://insights.blackcoffer.com/how-we-forecast-future-technologies/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in a mature operating system.</a> Even as time passes, the performance <a href="https://insights.blackcoffer.com/difference-between-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-statistics-and-data-mining/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">characteristics of any machine</a>, product, or operating system are normally improved in small continuous increments over time.</p>



<p>Advance in technology is usually nothing more than an accumulation of small advances not worth introducing individually to make a significant change in total technology. Forecast future technologies no matter how accurate – unless they eventually influence action.</p>
